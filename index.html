FAQ

Quick answers to common questions.

How does DiffGoblin detect changes?

We take a full-page screenshot of your URL every day and compare it pixel-by-pixel against the previous version. When pixels change, our AI reads the diff and summarizes what happened in plain English.

Can I monitor pages behind a login?

Not yet — that's on the roadmap. Right now we monitor any publicly accessible URL. Great for pricing pages, landing pages, TOS, and competitor sites.

What counts as a "monitored URL"?

Each unique URL you add counts as one. Our Scout plan includes 5, Pro includes 25, and Team includes 100. You can swap URLs anytime.

How is this different from Visualping or ChangeTower?

Most change monitors just highlight what changed. DiffGoblin adds an AI summary that tells you why it matters — "Acme raised Starter from $29 to $39/mo, shortened trial from 14 to 7 days." Context, not just pixels.
