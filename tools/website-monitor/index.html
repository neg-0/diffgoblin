Frequently asked questions

Is there really a free tier?
Yes. Monitor 1 URL with daily checks and email alerts. No credit card, no trial period, no catch. We want you to see the value before you pay for more URLs.
How is this different from Visualping or ChangeTower?
DiffGoblin focuses on visual diffs (screenshot-based), not HTML diffs. This means we catch changes that text-based monitors miss: layout shifts, image swaps, design changes, and dynamic content. We also include AI-powered change summaries so you don't have to squint at a diff image.
What kind of pages can I monitor?
Any publicly accessible webpage. Pricing pages, product pages, terms of service, job boards, landing pages, regulatory filings — if it has a URL and loads in a browser, we can monitor it.
How quickly will I get alerts?
We check monitored URLs once every 24 hours. When a change is detected, you'll receive an email within minutes. Paid plans include more frequent checks (every 6 hours or hourly).
Can I monitor pages behind a login?
Not yet. We're working on authenticated monitoring for paid plans. For now, we support any publicly accessible URL.
